Who can apply for Schengen Visa?

Anyone who meets the criteria set by the Schengen agreement can apply for a Schengen Visa.

How long is a Schengen visa valid for?

A Schengen visa can be valid for up to 90 days within a 180-day period.

How much does a Schengen visa cost?

The cost of a Schengen visa is typically around €80 for adults and €40 for children.

When should I apply for a Schengen visa?

You should apply for a Schengen visa at least 15 days before your planned trip.

What documents are included in my Visa?

Your visa will include details of your travel dates, the countries you can visit, and any specific conditions.

When should I apply for a Schengen visa?

Apply for your visa as soon as you have finalized your travel plans, ideally 3-6 months before your trip.

What do I do if my visa was rejected?

If your visa is rejected, you can appeal the decision or reapply with additional information or documentation.

What is ETIAS and do I need to get one?

ETIAS is an entry requirement for visa-exempt travelers to the Schengen Area. Check if you need it based on your nationality.
